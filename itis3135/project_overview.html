Project Overview

  1. Application and it's purpose: This application is a website for a tutoring business called AJ Tutoring. The purpose of this website is to provide customers with information about AJ Tutoring and how to book their services.
  2. Intended users of the website: The intended users of this website are parents of k-12 students, seeking tutoring for their child.
  3. Overview of the content of the website: This website will contain 5 pages, an index page, an about page, a testimonials page, a pricing page, and a contact page. Across these pages, customers will learn about the purpose of the business, qualifactions of the owner, pricing for their services, and how to book their services.

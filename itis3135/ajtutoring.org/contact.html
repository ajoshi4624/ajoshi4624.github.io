Frequently Asked Questions

How do you customize lessons for each student?

We start by assessing each student's current knowledge and learning style. Based on this, we create tailored lesson plans that focus on their unique needs and goals.

Can I request tutoring for a specific subject or topic?

Absolutely! We specialize in various subjects and can provide focused tutoring sessions on any specific topics or areas where help is needed.

What is the cancellation policy for tutoring sessions?

We understand that schedules can change. You can cancel or reschedule a session up to 24 hours in advance without any penalty. For cancellations within 24 hours, a fee may apply.

What subjects do you offer tutoring for?

We offer tutoring in a variety of subjects, including Math, Science, English, History, and more. Feel free to contact us to ask about any specific subject you're interested in!

How do I schedule a tutoring session?

You can schedule a session by contacting us through the 'Contact Us' page or directly emailing us. We’ll work with you to find a time that fits your schedule!

What are your tutoring rates?

Our rates vary depending on the subject and grade level. For middle schoolers, the rate is $50 per hour, and for high schoolers, it’s $65 per hour. Discounts may apply for package deals.
